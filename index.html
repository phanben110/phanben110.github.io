About me

I hold a Master’s degree in Computer Science from National Cheng Kung University (NCKU), Taiwan. My research interests span Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Deep Learning, with applications in Natural Language Processing and Computer Vision. I focus on optimizing Large Language Models (LLMs) and other deep learning architectures for efficient deployment on edge devices and servers, aiming to balance accuracy, scalability, and computational efficiency.

Originally from the picturesque Huong River region in central Vietnam, I earned my Bachelor's degree from Danang University of Science and Technology. I also completed the Vingroup AI Engineer Training Program, where I worked on practical AI applications and system-level engineering.

My passion for intelligent systems began with robotics, and I participated in both national and international competitions, including ABU Robocon 2020.

I’m always open to collaborative projects, research opportunities, and innovative ideas. Feel free to contact me via email, visit my GitHub, or download my CV.
